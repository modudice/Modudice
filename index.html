ModuDice

HOW TO PLAY:

Use your arrow keys to ROLL around the die.

When you roll onto a square the number on the top of the die gets added to the number on the square, modulo 7.

Match the color on the square with the color displayed on that side of the die to guarantee a 7.

Rack up points and additional moves by making 7's, and get even greater bonuses when you combo multiple 7's in a row!
